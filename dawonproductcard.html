KEY MATERIALS

STAINLESS STEEL

Stainless steel is a corrosion resistant iron alloy, Dawon uses 316 L stainless steel. Known for its high wear & tear property, this material is used for shafts, bearings, and rope lock housings. Just ideal choice for high performance weighted jump ropes.

ALUMINIUM

Aluminum is a lightweight metal, Dawon uses 6606 aluminium alloy. Known for its excellent mechanical properties, this material is used for handles, providing lightweight handles for agility & speed.

SPRING STEEL

Spring steel is a steel alloy with high elasticity. Dawon uses AISI 1095 steel for withstanding significant stress before permanently deforming. Lock mechanism hooks and retainer rings are made from this material, retaining high performance during rigorous jump rope routines.
